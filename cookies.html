Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-02

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to help sites function and learn how they are used.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential cookies for core features (e.g., remembering basic UI state).
  • Analytics (aggregated, non-identifying) to improve site performance.

Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use localStorage in games to save best scores. This data stays on your device.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies in your browser settings. Blocking cookies may impact functionality.
