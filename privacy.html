Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-02

HyperGameZ operates a free HTML5 gaming platform. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle data.

What We Collect

  • Contact information you submit through forms (e.g., name, email). This is used to reply to your inquiries.
  • Local device storage used by the games to save your best scores (via localStorage). Scores never leave your device unless you share them.
  • Basic analytics in aggregate (e.g., page views) to improve the site. No precise location or sensitive categories.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the site and games.
  • To respond to messages and provide support.
  • To improve features, balance gameplay, and fix bugs.

Local Storage & Scores

Games save high scores on your browser using localStorage. You can clear these anytime in the Games hub (button: “Clear Local Scores”) or by clearing your browser site data.

Cookies

We use only minimal, essential cookies for core site functionality and anonymous analytics. See our Cookie Policy.

Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards for data in transit and at rest. No method is 100% secure; please use a strong, unique email password if you contact us.

Children

This site is for audiences 18+. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
